Relax & Breath

Deep Breathing

Deep breathing in meditation offers several benefits: it helps reduce stress by calming the nervous system, improves focus and mental clarity by increasing oxygen flow to the brain, and enhances emotional stability by promoting relaxation. Additionally, deep breathing can lower heart rate and blood pressure, making it an effective tool for overall well-being. Regular practice can also lead to better sleep and increased mindfulness.

4-7-8 Technique

The 4-7-8 breathing technique, also known as "relaxing breath," is a simple practice that helps reduce anxiety, manage stress, and promote better sleep. By inhaling for 4 seconds, holding the breath for 7 seconds, and exhaling slowly for 8 seconds, it calms the nervous system and lowers heart rate. This technique increases oxygenation, helping to release tension and create a sense of inner peace. Regular practice can improve emotional resilience and overall mental health.
